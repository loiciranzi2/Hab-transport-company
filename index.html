About Us

HAB Transport Company is a leading transport and logistics company based in Kigali, Rwanda. We specialize in food distribution, cargo transportation, logistics support and delivery services. Our commitment to reliability, safety and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted partner for businesses across Rwanda.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To become Rwanda's most trusted transport and logistics company, delivering reliable, efficient, innovative and customer-focused transport solutions across Rwanda and the East African region.

Our Mission
